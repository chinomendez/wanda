Wanda relies on the Wonderflow's design language and principles and it helps designers and developers to keep brand consistency across projects and design assets. There are many resources shared across the teams like company logos, assets and icons. Here you can find everything related to the Wonderflow brand.

## Anatomy

Optical kerning, refined weight and defined clear space, as well as delineated placement in relation to other content help to make it as instantly recognizable as possible at all sizes and in all contexts.

## Logo formats

At Wonderflow we use three different version of the company logo based on the situation.

The full logo is used when the brand communication is strong and prominent. It needs a big white space area around in order to keep the information readable.
